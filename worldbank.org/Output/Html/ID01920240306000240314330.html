5. Bids must be accomplished by bid bond of Ethiopian birr 80,000 (Eighty thousand) for Lot one and 7,000.00 (Seven thousand) for Lot Two or 87,000 (eighty-seven thousand) for both lots in the form of UN Conditional bank guarantee or certified payment order (C.P.O)

7. Bidders should submit their technical and financial offers in wax-sealed envelope to the Office starting from the date announced on the Ethiopian Herald.

8. Bid will be closed in the presence of bidders or their legal representative on the March 27, 2024, at 2: 00 PM (Afternoon) & opened on the same date at 2: 30 PM (Afternoon) in the presence of bidder’s representative, who choose to attend, at the address below.

9. Late bids will be rejected.

10. Bidders may obtain further information from Bole Sub City Design and Construction Work Administrative and Finance Pool Office.
